Synonyms: Erythrina Sublobata Roxb., Erythrina Glabrescens ( English Name: Description: A Medium-sized, Deciduous Tree, Up To 18 M Tall, Branches Armed With White Or Pale Yellow Prickles, Young Parts, Under Surface Of Leaflets And Inflorescence Softly Tomentose. Leaflets Rhomboid-ovate, Or Obliquely Deltoid, 7.5-20.0 Cm Long And Broad, Deep Green And Glabrous Above, Glaucous And Matted With Grey, Cottony, Pubescence. Fruit A Pod, 12.5-15.0 Cm Long, Torulose, 2-5 Seeded, Black. Flowering Period: January-April Habit: Village Thickets And Forests. Distribution: Chittagong Uses: Used As Fence In Crop Fields.
