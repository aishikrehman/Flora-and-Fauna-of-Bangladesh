Family: Anacardiaceae Genus: Spondias Species: Spondias Pinnata Author: (L.f.) Kurz Synonyms: Mangifera Pinnata L. F., Spondias Mangifera Willd. English Name: Hog Plum Description: A Medium-sized To Large Tree, Glabrous, Deciduous With A Pleasant Smell (from Leaves And Twigs). Bark Greyish, Smooth But Often With Horizontal Wrinkles And Vertical Fissures, Blaze In Reddish-brown.
