Thiselton Dyer, English Name: The Eng Tree Description: A Medium To Large, Deciduous To Semi-deciduous Tree With Straight Bole And Stout Branches, Up To 35 M Tall And 2.5-3.5 M In Girth, Bark Dark Grey, Thick, Rough, And Longitudinally Fissured. Young Shoots Covered With Dense Stellate Tomentum, Sometimes Glabrous. Inflorescence Of Axillary, Simple Or Branched Racemes, The Lowest Flowers Often Distinctly Pedicelled, Peduncle Stellate Pubescent, Bracteoles 1.0-1.8 Cm Long, Linear-lanceolate, Caducous.
