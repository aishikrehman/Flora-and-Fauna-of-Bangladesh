Family: Araliaceae Genus: Macropanax Species: Macropanax Undulatum Author: (G. Don) Seem. Synonyms: Hedera Undulata G. Don, Macropanax Concinus Miq. English Name: Description: A Medium-sized Tree, Up To 15 M Tall, Bark Nearly Smooth, Pale-grey With Large Prominent Raised Lenticels. Leaves Digitately 3-5 Foliolate, Petiole 10-15 Cm Long, Leaflets Ovate-oblong To Narrowly Lanceolate, Cuneate To Rounded And Often Sub-oblique At The Base, Caudate-acuminate, Lateral Nerves 5-7 Pairs, Arcuate, Petiolules 1-3 Cm Long. Inflorescence Of Umbel, In A Terminal Panicle, Glabrous, Ascending. Bracts Caducous, Pedicels 3-6 Mm Long, Elongating In Fruits. Flowers About 4 Mm Across, Greenish-yellow. Ovary 2-celled, Style United Into A Column. Fruits Ovoid, About 5 Mm Long. Flowering Period: August-February Habit: Evergreen Forests.
