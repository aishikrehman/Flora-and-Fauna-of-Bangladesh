Family: Araliaceae Genus: Brassaiopsis Species: Brassaiopsis Palmata Author: Kurz Synonyms: English Name: Description: A Small, Sparingly Branched, Prickly Tree, Young Parts Tomentose With Rusty Stellate Scurf.
