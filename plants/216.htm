Family: Combretaceae Genus: Anogeissus Species: Anogeissus Sericea Author: Brandis Synonyms: English Name: Description: Large And Medium Sized Tree, Young Branches And Leaves Rusty Silky. Leaves Alternate, Many, Elliptic-suborbicular, Or Orbicular, Less Than 2 Cm Long. Flowers In Dense Globose Heads, Solitary, Or Subterminal Peduncles, Yellow To Brownish Yellow. Calyx Tube Compressed, 2-winged, Attenuate Above The Ovary, Limb Long Campanulate, Pubescent, Calyx Cup Semi-persistence. Stamens Exerted. Fruits Glabrous, Wings Of Fruits Jagged At The Apex Only. Flowering Period: December-April Habit: Distribution: Sylhet Uses: It Is Used As A Timber-wood Plant.
