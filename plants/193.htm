Family: Celastraceae Genus: Kokoona Species: Kokoona Littoralis Author: Laws. Synonyms: English Name: Description: Trees. Leaves Elliptic, Ovate Or Lanceolate, 4.5-15.5 X L.5-7 Cm, Acuminate, Base Cuneate, Chartaceous To Coriaceous, Margins Entire Or Slightly Recurved; Nerves 5-8 Pairs; Petioles Up To 3 Cm. Flowers In Axillary Racemes. Bracteoles Deltoid, Small, Acute, Denticulate. Sepals 5-lobed, Slightly Denticulate. Petals Broadly Ovate, Elliptic. Stamens Filamentous; Anthers With Prominent Connective. Ovary Triangular; Ovules 8-10 In Each Locule. Fruits Capsular, 13-18 X 3-6 Cm, Loculicidally Dehiscing, 3-valved. Seeds 6-10 In Each Locule, Winged Flowering Period: September-January Habit: Hill Forests.
