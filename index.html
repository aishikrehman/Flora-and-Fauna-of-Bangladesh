plant species identification module


This online digital database is the first of its kind and easily searchable to aid in the identification of plant species in Bangladesh. The Bangladesh Forest Department (BFD) and the Bangladesh National Herbarium (BNH) under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) developed this web based application with technical assistance from Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO).
